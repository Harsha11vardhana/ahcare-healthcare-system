Ayushman Bharat — PMJAY
Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana
Central
✅ Eligibility
  • BPL families in Mandya district
  • SECC 2011 listed households
  • Income below ₹2.5 lakh / year
  • Construction workers, domestic helpers

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • BPL / AAY Ration Card
  • Income Certificate (Tahsildar)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • PMJAY HHD Number / e-Card
🎁 Benefits
  • ₹5,00,000 cover / family / year
  • 1,929+ procedures covered
  • ICU, surgery, cancer, cardiac
  • Pre & post hospitalization
  • Cashless at Adichunchanagiri

  1. Visit hospital Reception
  2. Show Aadhaar + Ration card
  3. CSC operator checks PMJAY portal
  4. e-Card generated on-spot
  5. Cashless treatment begins

Arogya Karnataka
Karnataka Universal Health Coverage
State
✅ Eligibility
  • Karnataka resident (domicile cert)
  • Not covered under PMJAY / ESIC
  • BPL / APL families of Mandya
  • Income ≤ ₹5 lakh / year (APL)
  • Unorganized sector workers
📄 Documents Required
  • Karnataka domicile certificate
  • Aadhaar (all family members)
  • BPL / APL Ration Card
  • Income Certificate (Tahsildar)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
🎁 Benefits
  • BPL: ₹5,00,000 / year
  • APL: ₹1,50,000 / year
  • 1,600+ treatments covered
  • Dialysis, cancer, cardiac
  • Adichunchanagiri empanelled
📋 How to Apply
  1. Visit Mandya District Hospital
  2. Fill Arogya Karnataka form
  3. Submit documents at counter
  4. Biometric enrollment done
  5. Smart card issued in 7 days
